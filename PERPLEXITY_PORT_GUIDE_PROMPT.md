## PROMPT FOR PERPLEXITY:

```
Create a comprehensive cruise port guide for [PORT NAME], [COUNTRY] with the following information. Use British English spelling and factual tone. Do NOT include price symbols (£, €, $) or emojis.

1. BASIC INFORMATION
- Country:

- Overview paragraph about the cruise port, its size and location
- Which cruise terminals exist and where they are
- What shuttle services are available
- How far is the walk from port to city centre

4. GETTING AROUND
- How passengers get from ship to city
- Public transport options (metro, buses, trams)
- Taxi availability and info
- Walking distances
- Hop-on hop-off bus information if available

5. TRANSPORT CONNECTIONS
Airport:
- Airport name and code
- Distance from cruise port
- Transport options (taxi, bus, train) with journey times

Trains:
- Main railway station name
- Where trains go to/from
- Local train hubs

Cruise Lines: Which major cruise lines use this port regularly

6. TOP 6 ATTRACTIONS (Must-see sights for cruise passengers)
For EACH attraction provide ALL of the following:
- Name
- 2-3 sentence description of the attraction
- Duration: Suggested visit time in format "X to Y hours for a relaxed pace"
- 3 Insider Tips: Practical advice (e.g. best time to visit, booking tips, what to prioritize, photo spots, what to skip)
- Highlights: 3-5 from this list: Shopping, Food, History, Photography, Architecture, Gardens, Views, Culture, Art, Family-friendly, Religious, Museums, Markets, Beaches, Nature
- Good For: 2-4 from this list: First-time visitors, History buffs, Families, Photographers, Architecture lovers, Foodies, Active travelers, Art lovers, Culture seekers, Couples

7. NEAREST BEACH
- Beach name
- Description (2-3 sentences)
- How to get there from the port with distance/time
- Facilities (sunbeds, restaurants, toilets)
- Best for (families/swimming/watersports/quiet relaxation)

8. TOP 4 FOOD VENUES
For each provide:
- Name
- Type (Market/Restaurant/Tapas Bar/Food Hall/Cafe)
- Description (what they're known for)
- Location tip (e.g. "5 minute walk from cathedral")

9. LOCAL TIPS (6-8 practical tips)
Provide insider tips covering:
- Booking tickets in advance (which attractions need pre-booking)
- Safety and pickpockets (specific areas to watch)
- Timing and queues (best times to visit popular spots)
- Transport advice (what works best for cruise passengers)
- Money (cash vs card, tipping customs)
- Local customs (siesta times, dining hours, dress codes for churches)
- Things to avoid (tourist traps, overpriced areas)
- Quick wins (easy things visitors often miss)

10. WEATHER DATA

Jan: High °C, Low °C, Rain mm, Sunny days per month, Sea temp °C

Nov: High °C, Low °C, Rain mm, Sunny days per month, Sea temp °C
Dec: High °C, Low °C, Rain mm, Sunny days per month, Sea temp °C

Best time to visit:
- Overall best months and why
- Peak season (pros and cons)
- Quieter months (pros and cons)
- Recommendation for first-time cruise visitors

11. FAQ (10 QUESTIONS - IMPORTANT FOR SEO)
Provide 10 commonly asked questions that cruise passengers search for. Answer each in 2-3 sentences.

REQUIRED questions (adapt wording to the port):
1. Is [PORT NAME] walkable from the cruise port?
2. How long do you need in [PORT NAME]?
3. Is English widely spoken in [PORT NAME]?
4. Is [PORT NAME] safe for tourists?
5. Is [PORT NAME] expensive?
6. Do I need cash in [PORT NAME] or can I use card?
7. Should I book a ship excursion or explore independently?
8. What is the best thing to do in [PORT NAME] on a cruise day?
9. Is [PORT NAME] wheelchair accessible / good for limited mobility?
10. Can you do [NEARBY DAY TRIP] from [PORT NAME] on a cruise day?

For question 10, choose the most popular day trip from this port (e.g. Pompeii from Naples, Seville from Cadiz, Montserrat from Barcelona).

12. PRACTICAL INFO
- Best time to visit (1 sentence)
- Cruise terminal names (list)
- Nearest airport name and distance
- Visa requirements (for UK passport holders)
- Emergency number
- Tipping custom (brief)
- Siesta/shop closing times if applicable

13. TIME REQUIRED ESTIMATES
Provide realistic time estimates for the main sights:
- [Attraction 1]: X to Y hours for a relaxed pace
- [Attraction 2]: X to Y hours for a relaxed pace
- [Attraction 3]: X to Y hours for a relaxed pace
- [Attraction 4]: X to Y hours for a relaxed pace

Then provide a summary: "For a standard cruise day of 8 to 10 hours ashore, [realistic itinerary suggestion]."

Format all data clearly with British English (centre not center, organised not organized). Use "to" not em-dashes (2 to 3 hours, not 2–3 hours). No price symbols or emojis.
```
